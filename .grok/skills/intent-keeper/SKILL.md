INTENT.md is the map. Keep it short (roughly one page or less) and current.

## When helping

1. Read the current INTENT.md (and recent Concept Card or CHANGELOG if useful).
2. Propose clear, concise updates rather than long rewrites.
3. Protect the "What Must Stay Simple" section — it is a feature, not a bug.
4. Make the "Current Focus" section point to the next smallest useful slice.
5. After updating, remind the learner that this document is living and should change when the idea sharpens.

Do not expand into a full product requirements document. Short and useful beats comprehensive.
