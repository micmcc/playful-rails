description: Turn playtest notes or feelings into clear learning and next experiments. Use right after the learner has played a build or prototype.
---

Help turn "I played it" into useful direction.

## Process

1. Ask for (or accept) raw notes: what felt good, what felt confusing or boring, any surprises.
2. Reflect back the strongest signals in plain language.
3. Propose 2–5 concrete next experiments (small changes or questions to answer).
4. Suggest a short CHANGELOG entry and any needed update to INTENT.md.
5. Protect scope — prefer cutting or simplifying over adding when things feel muddy.

Celebrate what worked. Be honest but kind about what did not. Keep the next steps small enough to do in one short session.
