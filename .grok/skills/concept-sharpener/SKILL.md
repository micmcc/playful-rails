---
name: concept-sharpener
description: Turn a rough game idea into a tight Concept Card and suggest the smallest possible playable proof-of-concept. Use after brainstorming when a direction looks interesting.
---

Take a fuzzy or favorite idea and make it concrete enough to test.

## Output a short Concept Card

- **Name** (working title is fine)
- **Core fantasy / player goal** — what the player gets to feel or do
- **3–5 key moments or mechanics**
- **What must stay simple** (deliberate constraints)
- **Why this feels worth trying right now**

Then suggest **one tiny playable slice** (the smallest thing we could build or paper-prototype that would let us feel whether the idea has spark).

Keep the whole card short. Prefer clarity over completeness.
If the idea is still too broad, ask one or two focused questions to tighten it before writing the card.
