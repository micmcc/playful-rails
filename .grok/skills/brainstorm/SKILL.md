Help the learner generate game ideas in a playful, low-pressure way.

## How to run

1. Ask (or recall) a few light constraints they care about:
   - Genre or vibe they like
   - Rough play length
   - Art style preference (or "keep it simple")
   - Anything they definitely do *not* want
2. Generate 8–12 rough ideas. Keep each to 1–3 sentences. Mix familiar and slightly surprising ideas.
3. Invite them to mark 2–3 favorites and say why (even one sentence is enough).
4. Offer to move a favorite into a Concept Card (hand off to concept-sharpener) or keep exploring.

Stay encouraging. Quantity and energy matter more than polish at this stage.
Do not jump straight into full specs or implementation.
